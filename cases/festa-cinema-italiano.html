SEC.01
Context
+ StrategyContexto + estratégia

The Festa do Cinema Italiano is an annual festival dedicated to contemporary Italian cinema, with screenings across multiple Portuguese cities. The communication team is small, requiring full ownership of content — from strategy to execution — across all platforms.

The strategy focused on adapting language to each channel: editorial and cinephile content on Instagram, native trend-driven formats on TikTok, a more institutional tone on Facebook and the website, and newsletters (MailerLite) for audience loyalty.

"Managing 5 platforms with a team of 2 requires editorial clarity — knowing what to say on each channel and what not to replicate."

Content production in two languages (Portuguese and Italian) added an extra layer of cultural and linguistic precision.

SEC.02
ExecutionExecução

Work included ground-up content creation: photomontages, feed and story design, video editing for Reels and TikTok, and copywriting in Portuguese and Italian. TikTok videos were created natively for the platform — not repurposed from Instagram — with appropriate language and rhythm.

This experience consolidated the ability to manage the complete communication of a cultural event from scratch — strategy, content, design, and coverage — without relying on a large team. The diversity of platforms and formats reinforced the skill of adapting tone and language per channel.

"Introducing a classic film festival to TikTok is an exercise in cultural translation — not simplification, but recontextualization."

Bilingual content production (Portuguese and Italian) proved to be a competitive advantage, allowing the festival to communicate directly with the Italian community in Portugal and with international partners.
