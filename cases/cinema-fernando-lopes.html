SEC.01
Context
+ StrategyContexto + estratégia

An independent cinema competes with far more limited resources than major distributors. The strategy combined digital communication and press relations — with consistent messaging but different languages — ensuring that the premiere of "O Jovem Xamã" gained visibility in relevant cultural media.

"An independent cinema needs to be precise and consistent, not noisy. The quality of the message always outweighs the volume."

Creating the brand identity for the cycle "O Best of Que Ninguém Pediu" came from the need to give a distinct personality to a curation project, distinguishing it from the venue's general communication.

This experience introduced a new dimension: coordinating digital communication and press relations simultaneously. Managing both with consistent but differently styled messages proved valuable for multi-channel brand management.

"Creating an identity for a cinema cycle is, above all, an exercise in clarity: what do we want people to feel before they know what they're going to see?"

The integrated management of social media, newsletters, and press relations demonstrated the importance of coordinated communication, where each channel reinforces the other without excessive repetition.
