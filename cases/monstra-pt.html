SEC.01
Context
+ StrategyContexto + estratégia

The MONSTRA — Lisbon Animation Festival is Portugal's leading animation film festival, featuring screenings, workshops, exhibitions, and industry events. When I joined in 2025, the digital presence needed a more consistent editorial line and a strategic approach to growth.

The strategy was built on two pillars: consistent year-round content to build anticipation, and platform-native storytelling — Instagram for community, LinkedIn for institutional reach, and Letterboxd for film enthusiasts.

"With 4 months of campaign and €300 in paid ads, the goal was to prove that a small budget, well applied, can generate disproportionate results."

In 2026, we introduced paid social on Meta Suite for the first time — a €300 budget focused on amplifying the best-performing organic content during peak festival weeks.

SEC.02
ExecutionExecução

Management was full-cycle: monthly content planning over a four-month campaign, content creation (copy, design, photo, and video), scheduling via Meta Suite, and weekly performance monitoring. During the festival, real-time coverage included daily posts and stories, community management, and coordination of photo and video teams.

Meta Ads

In 4 months, MONSTRA's Instagram generated over 2.5 million views and grew by 3,500 followers — with a total paid ads budget of €300. The cost per follower gained was under €0.09.

"2.5 million views with €300. The ROI doesn't come from the budget — it comes from the quality of the content that budget amplifies."

The introduction of paid social confirmed that paid amplification works best when organic content is already validated. The best-performing ads were boosted posts — not pieces created specifically for paid campaigns.
